# Impeccable

## Design Context

### Users
Curious people of all ages exploring the solar system for fun and learning — at a desk or on a phone. The job: wander the planets, play with time and scale, pick up facts along the way.

### Brand Personality
Wondrous, precise, playful. A "mission control" for everyone — scientific instrument aesthetics softened with delight (✨ discover a planet, time travel via date picker).

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark cosmic theme (near-black `#020208`, starfield) — the 3D scene is the hero; UI floats above it as translucent blurred panels (`rgba(10,14,28,.78)` + blur). Accent: soft starlight blue `#6fa8ff`. Uppercase letter-spaced headings like instrument labels. Never let chrome compete with the sky.

### Design Principles
1. The canvas is the product — panels must stay out of its way, especially on small screens.
2. Touch is a first-class input: 44px targets, bottom-anchored controls within thumb reach.
3. Adapt, don't amputate: every desktop control exists on mobile.
4. Information density over decoration; facts read like a flight manual, not a brochure.
